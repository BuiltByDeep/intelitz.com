Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical project timeline?

↓

Timelines vary, but a typical project takes 3-6 months. We provide a detailed schedule after the initial discovery phase.

How do you handle project communication?

↓

We use a dedicated Slack channel, regular video calls, and a project management tool to ensure you're always up-to-date.

Do you offer post-launch support?

↓

Yes, we offer flexible support and maintenance packages to keep your product secure, updated, and performing optimally.

How do you choose the right technology for my project?

↓

Our choice of technology is driven by your project's specific needs, including scalability, performance requirements, and budget. We conduct a thorough analysis to select the most suitable and future-proof stack for your product.

Who owns the intellectual property (IP) of the software?

↓

You do. Upon final payment, the intellectual property rights for the source code and design of the software we develop for you are fully transferred to your company.

How is the project cost determined?

↓

Project costs are based on the scope, complexity, and timeline. We offer two main models: a fixed-price model for well-defined projects and a time-and-materials model for more flexible, evolving requirements. We provide a detailed estimate after our initial discovery phase.
